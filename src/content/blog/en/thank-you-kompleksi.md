After cognitive science transferred to the Faculty of Arts at the turn of the year, the psychology discipline transferred to the Faculty of Medicine. The transfers are at this stage still administrative, meaning students of both disciplines still hang out mostly at Siltavuorenpenger under a common roof. The actual "separation" is ahead at the latest in late summer before the new academic year begins. The relationship between cognitive science's student organization Intelligenzia and psychology's student organization Kompleksi has been warm until now: many joint events have been organized during the cohabitation and students still meet each other constantly in shared courses and outside of them. In the near future, the relationship between student organizations will become a long-distance one physically, but cooperation will surely continue in the future as well.
