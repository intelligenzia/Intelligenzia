## Name of the Register

Intelligenzia ry Member Register

## Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

Personal data is processed for managing association membership and communication with members. The legal basis for processing is:

- Performance of membership agreement (member register)
- Consent of the data subject (marketing communications)
- Legal obligation (maintaining member register as required by the Finnish Associations Act)

## Personal Data Processed

We collect and process the following personal data:

- Name
- Email address
- Membership start date
- Membership payment information

## Data Retention Period

Member data is retained for the duration of membership and thereafter for the period required by accounting legislation (6 years from the end of the financial year).

## Data Disclosure and Transfer

Personal data is not disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed:

- To authorities to fulfill legal obligations
- To payment processing services (Stripe) for processing membership fees

Stripe processes payment data according to their own privacy practices. More information: [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy)

## Register Protection

Register data is protected with appropriate technical and organizational measures. Access to data is limited to only those persons whose job duties require it.

## Rights of the Data Subject

The data subject has the right to:

- **Right of access**: Know what personal data has been stored about them
- **Right to rectification**: Request correction of incorrect data
- **Right to erasure**: Request deletion of their data (taking into account statutory retention obligations)
- **Right to restriction of processing**: Request restriction of personal data processing
- **Right to object**: Object to processing of personal data
- **Right to withdraw consent**: Withdraw given consent at any time

Requests regarding the exercise of rights should be sent to hallitus@intelligenzia.fi.

## Cookies

Our website uses technically necessary cookies that enable basic site functions such as login. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Members will be notified of significant changes by email.
